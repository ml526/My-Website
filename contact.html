About Me

Hello! My name is Ming Li Lim, a full-time college student, a budding entrepreneur, and an experienced crocheter. A little about me, I have an older sister and two younger brothers, and a secret pet dragon. I love to play video games and immersing myself in nature. I LOVE crochet. I’ve been crocheting for more than 6 years now. I am mostly self-taught, thanks to the glorious Internet. I created many gifts for my friends and family and don’t plan on stopping now. Crochet is a great hobby, but it does take patience, perseverance, and practice. However, don’t let them stop you! I believe everyone has the ability to crochet. I am still learning every time I pick up my hook and I always see amazing work from other people. I hope this site inspires you to pick up crochet or continue if you stopped, or learn something new. Saving the world one stitch at a time.
